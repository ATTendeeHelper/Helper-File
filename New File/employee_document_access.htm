Employee Document Access

The employee document access allows the HRM to restrict (grant or deny) employees access to a particular file.

To restrict employee access on files, click on Document Management at left side menu on your dashboard.


Fig 1.0


Fig 1.2
  • On the Employee Document Access tab the list of employees are displayed.
  • Select the employee to be granted or denied access and click on the Allow/Denny Access buttoon.
  • A small interface will be displayed see Image 1.2
  • You can now grant or deny access to employee to ADD, UPDATE, DELETE and VIEW documents.
  • Then click Submit and the access will automatically be granted or denied based on your selection.
