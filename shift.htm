SHIFT SCHEDULING

This is one of the most important module of ATTendee HRM,this allows the HRM to set shift schedules based on certain parameter or merits. The step below simplifies how shift is been done

On your dashboard, Click on the menu at the left corner then select Shift Scheduling from the list of options.



Fig 1.0 : Shift Scheduling

This module allows HR's to set

  • Shift timing
  • Shift Roaster
  • and also generate Shift Roaster report

Setting Shift Timing

To set time for staffs/personnel shift, click on the Create New Shift Time Button, as show in the diagram above



Then select the category (Morning, Afternoon or Night),
Set expecting Clock time (In/out)
Set Break Time (out/in)
if your settings is found satisfactory, click on the Create New Shift Time Button, as show in the diagram above
Shift time will be added to the Module automatically


Setting Shift Roaster

Shift Roaster allows HR's to know the staffs to be/are placed on shift. Hence monitoring staffs in and out. To set Shift roaster, click on the Shift Roaster Tab, as show in Fig 1.0 above

Click on the Create Shift Roaster Button, then drag set shift into the calendar
N:B Ensure to put the name of the Roaster



Once you are done, click on the Create Shift Roaster Button Roaster will automatically be update on the screen




Assigning Shift Assignment

Shift Assignment allows HR's to assign shifts to staffs by employee or by location. To set Shift roaster, click on the Shift Roaster Tab and select Shift Assignment

Step 1: Select Shift Roasted based on what you've set,
Step 2: Select Employee by (Department, Location, Individual, All staff),
Step 3: choose the last step based on step 2.



Once you are done, click on the Assign Shift Button Shift will also be automatically assigned



Generating Shift Roaster Report

To generate Shift Roaster Report, click on the Shift Roaster Report Tab, as show in Fig 1.0 above

Select from the option to use and Date to generate report. Click on the Generate Report Button, then you would see your report generate as shown in the image on the right side



Generated roaster report can be exported as an excel file for further usage.
